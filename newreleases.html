Biography


From rapper to producer, producer to audio engineer, Tha 5th Element truly is a One Man Band. Over 15 years in the making, Tha 5th Element has won the 2017 Las Vegas Hip-Hop Beat Battle Tournament, placed 3rd at the 2017 Las Vegas EDM Beat Battle Tournmant, and performed his songs all over the world in addition to having his music featured in the Mixed Martial Arts Podcast, The Co-Main Event Podcast.
